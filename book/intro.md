# Welcome to Foundations of Neural and Cognitive Modeling

This website contains the lab assignments for the course "Foundations of Neural and Cognitive Modeling" at the University of Amsterdam (Semester 1, Period 2, Year 2022), taught by professor Willem (Jelle) Zuidema with the help of the teaching assistant Simone Astarita.

You can access the assignments through the menu on the left. To open a lab assignment in Google Colab, go to the assignment page and hover your mouse over the rocket symbol (<img alt="rocket" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FortAwesome/Font-Awesome/6.x/svgs/solid/rocket.svg" width="18" height="18">) in the top right, then choose _Colab_.
